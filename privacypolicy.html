Privacy Policy

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how ("we," "our," or "us") collects, uses, and protects the personal information of users ("you" or "your") in our RFID Library System.

2. Information We Collect

We may collect and process the following types of personal information:

  • Personal Identification Information: Name, student ID, contact details, and other identifiers.
  • Library Usage Data: Details of books and materials borrowed, returned, and overdue, including timestamps.
  • RFID Data: RFID tag information associated with library items.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

  • Library Management: To manage borrowing, returning, and tracking of library materials.
  • Communication: To contact you regarding your library account, due dates, and library events.
  • Security and Anti-theft: To prevent and detect theft or loss of library materials.
  • Statistical Analysis: To analyze library usage patterns and improve our services.

4. Information Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your personal information to outside parties except:

  • Service Providers: Third-party service providers who assist us in operating our library system, subject to confidentiality agreements.
  • Legal Requirements: When required by law, regulation, or legal process to disclose the information.

5. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. These measures include:

  • Access Controls: Restricting access to personal information to authorized personnel only.
  • Encryption: Using encryption methods to protect sensitive data during transmission and storage.
  • Regular Audits: Conducting regular security audits to ensure the effectiveness of our data protection measures.

6. Data Retention

We retain your personal information for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes for which it was collected and to comply with legal and regulatory requirements. After that period, the information will be securely deleted or anonymized.

7. Your Rights

You have the following rights regarding your personal information:

  • Access: The right to request a copy of your personal information.
  • Correction: The right to request correction of any inaccurate or incomplete information.
  • Deletion: The right to request deletion of your personal information, subject to legal and regulatory constraints.
  • Objection: The right to object to the processing of your personal information under certain circumstances.

8. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on our website and/or communicated to you via email. The effective date of the latest version will be indicated at the top of the policy.

9. Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy or our data practices, please contact us.
